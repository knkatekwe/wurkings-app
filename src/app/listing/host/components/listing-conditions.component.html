Review the conditions for renting on Wurkings

Wurkings has the following requirements before all users can rent from you.

All Wurkings lesses must provide:
  • Confirmed Email address
  • Confirmed phone number
  • Payment Information
Before renting your listing, each lesse must agree to your:
  • Agree to your listing rules
  • Message you about their enquiry
  • Let you know how many hours/days/weeks they will need it
  • Confirm when they will collect the listing within 2 days
